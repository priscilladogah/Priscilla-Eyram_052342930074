HISTORY

The INTERNATIONAL CENTRAL GOSPEL CHURCH(I.C.G.C) is an Evangelical Charismatic Christian Church.It was officially inaugrated as a church on the 26th of February 1984, in Accra,Ghana with an intial membership of just about twenty people.From February 1984 to April 1986 the membership grew to about one hundred and eight(108) adults in a regular attendance.The early meetings of the church were held in several facilities,including classrooms,a private residence,a public hall, a science laboratory, a medical workshop and a cinema theatre. In May 1986, the church settled in a rented scout hall -the Baden Powell Memorial hall -which became its home for the next 10 years.During this ten years period,the membership rose to 4000. This period also saw aggressive missionary church planting activities with local assemblies established in almost all the major town and cities of ghana. Several other churches were also planted in cities in Europe and United states

  • The first congregation which was established in February 1984, now designated as the christ temple has directly planted over forty other churches out of the original congregation in Accra-Tema metropolis of Ghana alone.In 1988, the ICGC established a ministerial institute to train a new generation of leaders to carry out it vision. From the initial six month certificate in ministry, the college has developed into a premier private-owned university in Ghana known as the Central University College
  • Again in 1988, the church instituted an educational scholarship scheme, known as Central Aid to finance the education of selected needy student in pre-tertiary educational institutions.The scheme is now considered the largest non-governmental scholarship programme for students in pre-tertiary education in Ghana.
