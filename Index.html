THE FOUNDER

The founder of ICGC is PASTOR MENSAH OTABIL

Pastor Mensah Otabil is a Ghanaian thelogist, philanthropist, motivational speaker, enterpreneur.In 2015, Ghanaians voted him as the "most influencial person in Ghana", according to a list published by ETV Ghana. He is the founder and chancellor of Central University College.Pastor Mensah Otabil oversees the the network of ministeries of International Central Gospel Church worldwide.He also serve as the senior pastor of Christ temple(headquarters). His work has centered relevant to Africa through various conferences.
